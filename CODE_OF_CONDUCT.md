# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant, v2.1][cc].

In short: be respectful, assume good faith, and keep the discussion
focused on the work. Harassment, personal attacks, and discriminatory
language are not welcome here.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behaviour that violates this code,
please report it by emailing **ckumar392@users.noreply.github.com**. Reports will
be reviewed and acted on promptly and confidentially.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may warn, temporarily ban, or permanently ban any
contributor for behaviour they deem inappropriate, threatening,
offensive, or harmful — with or without prior warning, and at their
sole discretion.

[cc]: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/
